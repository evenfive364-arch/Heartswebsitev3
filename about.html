About
H.E.A.R.T.S.

H.E.A.R.T.S. Charitable Trust was born from a deep conviction that healing is not a clinical procedure — it is a living, relational, and culturally rooted experience.

We exist for those who need to be met exactly where they are: with dignity, warmth, and an understanding that their story — and the stories carried in their whakapapa — matters.

Our Vision

"

A world where every person, regardless of their history or the weight they carry, has access to healing that honours their full humanity — body, mind, wairua, and whakapapa.

What Sets Us Apart

Three pillars that
ground everything we do.

01

Culturally Grounded
Healing

We work through tikanga Māori, honouring whakapapa, whanaungatanga, and mātauranga Māori to support healing that is deeply rooted, meaningful, and aligned with identity. Our work does not strip people of who they are — it returns them to it.

02

Holistic,
Whole-Person Care

Our approach supports mind, body, and spirit — recognising that true wellbeing comes from addressing the full human experience, not just isolated challenges. We collaborate with doctors, psychologists, and psychiatrists to bring integrated care to every person we serve.

03

Equine-Led
Connection

Through equine-supported therapy, we utilise the horse-human connection to foster emotional regulation, trust, and resilience in a safe and powerful way. The horses do not perform — they respond honestly to what is present, creating a mirror that words alone cannot.
