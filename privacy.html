Privacy Statement

Donor Bill of Rights

The H.E.A.R.T.S. Charitable Trust is committed to protecting your privacy and your rights as a donor. As a charitable trust registered under the Charities Act 2005 (CC51224), we maintain high standards for the protection of data to ensure that donor information remains private and is only used for the purpose it was collected.

Protecting your privacy

In accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act, we at H.E.A.R.T.S. Charitable Trust maintain high standards for the protection of data. Your personal information is stored in secure databases in New Zealand. We follow strict security procedures in the storage and disclosure of your personal information.

This Statement is regularly reviewed and updated to meet legal requirements for privacy.

Personal information

'Personal information' is any information that can identify you. We collect and hold personal information from our donors, supporters, volunteers, contractors, and visitors to our programs. For example, if you are a donor, we would collect your name, address, and email to process your donations accurately.

We'll only gather your data when you supply it to us voluntarily. We use this information solely to fulfil your request(s).

Credit and debit card security

The H.E.A.R.T.S. Charitable Trust is PCI DSS compliant. We do not store credit or debit card details. All payment processing is handled through secure, compliant third-party providers.

Job applicants & volunteers

Information provided through job or volunteer applications is used solely for recruitment purposes and will not be shared with third parties without your consent.

The Donor Bill of Rights

As a donor to H.E.A.R.T.S. Charitable Trust, you have the right to:

  • Be informed of the organisation's mission and how donations are used
  • Know the identity of those serving on the governing board
  • Have access to the most recent financial statements
  • Be assured your gift will be used for the purposes for which it was given
  • Receive appropriate acknowledgement and recognition
  • Be assured that information about your donation is handled with respect and confidentiality
  • Expect that all relationships with individuals representing H.E.A.R.T.S. will be professional
  • Be informed whether those seeking donations are volunteers, employees, or hired contractors
  • Have the opportunity for your name to be deleted from mailing lists
  • Feel free to ask questions and receive prompt, truthful, and forthright answers

Complaints

If you have a concern about how your personal information has been handled, please contact us at horsesensetrust@gmail.com. We will respond promptly and work to resolve your concern.
