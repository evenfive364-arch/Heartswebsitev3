Programs

Our Offerings

We offer a variety of programs depending on the need and intention behind connecting with our horses.

Therapy sessions, personal growth, professional development, retreats and training workshops — each designed around the horse-human connection.

01

Fun Team Building

For Organisations & Groups

Ever played football with a horse? Spend the day in the fresh air, out in nature, with horses on a completely new level. You will find that people's personalities come out in unique ways, creating a foundation for new connections and a stronger bond between team members.

We have a vast range of activities we can do with your group and we are happy to tailor your visit, making this your own unique experience.

Sample Day

  • → Morning: Meet the herd & team activities
  • → 12:30–2:30: Lunch at a local café
  • → Afternoon: Group activities & reflection
  • → 4:30–5:00: Closing & photo opportunity

Early bird discount available — 20% off when booked 6 months in advance.

02

Workshops & Retreats

Personal & Collective Growth

The interconnectedness of horses and humans has been in relationship for thousands of years. In the 21st century, horses are willing partners to reconnect us in a meaningful way — for those who open to the interactive dialogue of their ability to help us heal.

Our workshops are a combination of different modalities, including systemic constellations, mindfulness and sound meditation with horses, and somatic awareness.

Their journey now, for some, is to carry us back to ourselves.

03

Mauri Supervision

Supportive & Developmental

"Life principle, life force, vital essence, special nature — the essential quality and vitality of a being or entity." — Māori Dictionary

Grounded in a theoretical framework, our supervision model believes the supervisee has the capacity to effectively resolve problems without interpretation. The supervisor serves as a collaborator, providing 25 years combined knowledge of Equine Supported Therapy and Psychology.

  • Time & priority management
  • Diversity & generational differences
  • Critical thinking & reflective practice

04

Session Framework

What to Expect

  • Two facilitators always present

    Providing emotional and physical safety for the client throughout.

  • Sessions outdoors

    Being outside in the presence of the herd slows the heart rate and creates an open, somatic experience.

  • No horse experience required

    No horsemanship skills involved. We can begin from the other side of the fence if needed.

  • One to ten sessions

    Sessions are one hour (first session 1.5 hrs). Number varies by intention — even a single session can restore clarity.

  • Full confidentiality

    Personal information is treated with respect and not shared with third parties without consent.

05

Financial Assistance

We work to make sessions accessible

ACC — Sensitive Claims (ISSC)

ACC's ISSC contract covers all costs for long-term therapy for clients who have experienced sexual violence. ACC also covers costs for those with a physical injury impacting mental health.

WINZ Disability Allowance

A weekly payment for ongoing costs due to a physical or mental disability — including therapy. You don't need to be on a benefit to qualify.

Victim Support Grant

Families of homicide victims and victims of sexual violence may be eligible for a financial grant covering counselling. Call 0800 842 846.

Oranga Tamariki

Financial assistance for counselling for foster carers and their tamariki. Call 0508 227 377.

Let us know which applies to you — we'll guide you through the process.
