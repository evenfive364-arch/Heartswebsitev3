Our Team

The horses are not tools. They are teachers, healers, and partners in this sacred work.

01 — Ngā Hōiho

Tomahawk

Protector · Teacher · Heart Horse

He is the perfect blend of charm and courage. With a calm and sweet nature, he wins hearts effortlessly, giving off an impression that he's as gentle as a lamb. But beneath that serene exterior lies a fierce protector — when it comes to his herd, Tomahawk is unwaveringly loyal and ready to rise to any occasion.

When Jenny went to buy him she asked him if he wanted to come home with her. He tucked his head under her arm. The rest is history…

02 — Ngā Hōiho

Mystique

Nurturer · Caregiver · Mother

A natural caregiver, Mystique is the gentle heart of the herd. As the mother of Cruz and two other foals, she carries with her a history of love and dedication. Her self-reliance shines through in the way she effortlessly communicates her desires and cares for herself with grace.

She models what healthy boundaries and quiet strength look like — a living teacher of self-worth and relational care.

03 — Ngā Hōiho

Ollie

Koroua · Elder · Heart Horse

At 33 years old, Ollie is our treasured Koroua — a horse with a lifetime of memories and achievements. Known for his exceptional kindness and gentle demeanour, he's a 650kg bundle of sweetness who has earned the love of everyone around him.

Retirement suits him beautifully as he enjoys a well-deserved, carefree life. Ollie's earlier years were filled with adventure as Jenny's main horse — together they hunted, evented, and trekked, with his last great journey being through the rugged Kaimanawa country alongside Aotearoa's wild horses.

If H.E.A.R.T.S. has a heart horse, it is unquestionably Ollie.

Founder & Director

Jenny

Founder · Facilitator · Equine Practitioner

Jenny founded H.E.A.R.T.S. from a deep personal and professional calling — to create a space where those who carry the heaviest weight can find their way back to themselves, their whakapapa, and to life.

Her work is rooted in tikanga Māori, equine-assisted practice, and systemic family constellation work. She holds the medicine circle as a sacred space — one where healing does not need to be spoken to be felt.

Jenny collaborates closely with doctors, psychologists, and psychiatrists to bring truly integrated, whole-person care to every person she serves.

Our Herd · Son of Mystique

Cruz

Born into the H.E.A.R.T.S. herd, Cruz carries the gentle wisdom of his mother Mystique in his blood. He has grown up surrounded by healing work, and this shows in his quiet confidence and easy presence with people of all ages.

Young and still finding his way, Cruz brings a sense of playfulness and curiosity to sessions — often the first to approach newcomers, lowering the barrier of unfamiliarity with a warm, generous spirit. He reminds us that healing can also be joyful.

"Cruz grew up watching healing happen. Now he is part of it."
