Support Our Work

H.E.A.R.T.S. Charitable Trust is a registered charitable organisation (CC51224) under the Charities Act 2005. Your koha — gift — directly supports equine-assisted healing for rangatahi Māori, survivors of trauma, and those in crisis.

Every donation helps us keep our herd, maintain our whenua, and ensure that cost is never a barrier to healing.

Volunteering

Join Us on the Kāpiti Coast

We are located just north of Ōtaki with stunning 360° views over the Tararua Ranges and the beautiful coastline — a space of healing and growth. We are building a sustainable, supportive, and holistic centre for horses and humans.

We welcome energetic, dedicated people to support us. Current volunteer activities include:

  • → Renovating buildings on-site
  • → Composting, recycling and sustainability projects
  • → General yard duties and animal care
  • → Professional skills: funding, accounting, marketing

To Apply

Send your CV and Motivation Letter to horsesensetrust@gmail.com and we'll be in touch.

We look for considerate team players with a can-do attitude, a respectful work ethic, and an interest in the horse-human connection. Experience with recycled materials, composting, or sustainable building is a bonus.

Outcomes Research

Adolescent Sexual Abuse & Trauma — EAGALA Model

Researchers found significant improvements in psychological distress and functioning after EAGALA Model intervention for adolescents who experienced sexual abuse. Journal of Child and Family Studies, 2013

Psychiatric Patients & Violent Behaviour

Hospitalized patients randomly assigned to EAGALA Model equine-assisted psychotherapy showed the largest decrease in violent behaviour compared to other interventions. Psychiatric Services, 2014

Student Stress Reduction

Students receiving EAGALA Model stress management showed significant reductions in stress, performing as well as other modalities including yoga and mindfulness. Journal of Creativity in Mental Health, 2017
